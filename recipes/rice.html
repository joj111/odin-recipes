Steamed Rice

Despriction

To get this delicious bowl of rice, you will need to do some simple actions to your rice before hand.

This delicious rice is best if it is Jasmine Rice.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Salt (or any seasoning(PS. You do not need seasoning for delicious rice.))
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash rice
  2. Fill with water
  3. Cook rice
  4. Season rice (if prefered)
