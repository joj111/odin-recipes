Instant Noodles

Description

These cup of noodles aren't your typical cup of noodles, they are from Japan.

Though they have the same preperation method, they are far more supperior than the exported cup of noodles here in the states.

Ingredients

  • Cup of Noodles CURRY
  • Water (boiling hot)

Steps

  1. Get the cup of noodles
  2. Put water in the noodles
  3. Wait 2-4 minutes (consistency preference)
